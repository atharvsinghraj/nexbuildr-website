FAQ

Common Questions

Who started NexBuildr?
+
NexBuildr was founded by Atharv Singh Raj, a young entrepreneur with a mission to build India's most powerful startup ecosystem for students and emerging founders. The team includes co-founders Hardik Dawar, Ayush Kumar, Anurag, and Pradyumna Saha — all driven by the same vision.
How does NexBuildr help me?
+
NexBuildr gives you access to AI tools to validate your idea, build your brand, and create pitch decks. You also get access to a global community of 400+ builders, a startup showcase to gain visibility, and direct networking with investors, mentors, and co-founders.
How is NexBuildr different?
+
Unlike generic platforms, NexBuildr is built specifically for student founders and early-stage builders. We combine AI tools, real-time community chat, a startup showcase, and a professional network — all in one place. We are free, accessible, and built by builders for builders.
Who should join?
+
Anyone with an idea, a business, or a desire to build something impactful. Whether you are a student with your first startup idea, a young business owner looking to scale, a freelancer building a personal brand, or a founder preparing for funding — NexBuildr is made for you.
Is NexBuildr free to use?
+
Yes. NexBuildr is completely free to join and use. Our core platform — including community chat, AI tools, startup showcase, and networking features — is available to all members at no cost. Premium features may be introduced in the future, but the core will always remain free.
